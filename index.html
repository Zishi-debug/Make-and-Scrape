When I dig into the cuteness in character, I find it is hard to ignore the sexuality behind it. I call it the male gaze in anime and manga culture.Different from the male gaze in Hollywood movies, it is not just sexual desire for female character , there is also a kind of palatonic love to godness and idol. There are three factor in this otaku sexuality:"dehumanization", "the absence of real sex", "the absence of others". As for the the absence of real sex, it is a kind of sex based on images and virtual world and characters. I am not sure how to use computational technologies to explore this concept, but basically I want to create a parallel universe just like the twisted world in "living with a mysterious gril".
